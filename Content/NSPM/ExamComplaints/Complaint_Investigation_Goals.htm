Examination Complaint Investigation Goals

Our main goal is to anticipate and resolve problems at the field level whenever possible. We expect the investigative process to:

  • Determine the real issues and work towards solutions
  • Reach agreements based on facts and complete necessary corrective action
  • Identify potential deficiencies in agency systems, methods, and procedures and effect correction
  • Identify examiner performance issues
  • Provide a written response from the Regional Director to the credit union within a 30-day timeframe on all appeals that involve material supervisory determinations
