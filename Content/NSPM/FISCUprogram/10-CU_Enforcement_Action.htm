Credit Union Enforcement Action

The NCUA defines a problem credit union as one that either the NCUA or the SSA rates a CAMEL 4 or 5. Either informal (Regional Director letter, DOR, LUA) or formal (C&D, removal, prohibition, etc.) enforcement action is necessary in all problem credit unions. In CAMEL 3, 4, or 5 credit unions, the supervisor will coordinate and consult with the ARDP and Regional Director for concurrence before proceeding with a formal enforcement action recommendation consistent with other parts of this manual and . The NCUA and the SSA will coordinate enforcement actions such as an LUA), C&D, conservatorship, or liquidation.
