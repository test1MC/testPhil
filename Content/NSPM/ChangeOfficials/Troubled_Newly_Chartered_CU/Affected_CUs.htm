Affected Credit Unions

  • Chartered within the past two years
  • For FCUs assigned a CAMEL composite rating of 4 or 5 by the NCUA or, for FISCUs, an equivalent of CAMEL 4 or 5 by either the NCUA (after an onsite contact) or its state supervisor; or
  • Credit unions granted assistance under §208 of the that remain outstanding and unextinguished
