Prompt Corrective Action

Prompt corrective action is designed to restore and improve the net worth of federally-insured credit unions. When a credit union is subject to PCA, the district examiner will monitor compliance to all applicable provisions of PCA contained in Part 702 of . This section of the NSPM establishes national policy and guidelines NCUA staff will follow when completing PCA related work assignments and examination/supervision of PCA related issues.

For more information, see NCUA Letter to Credit Unions 01-CU-01, Prompt Corrective Action (PCA) Implementation Information.
