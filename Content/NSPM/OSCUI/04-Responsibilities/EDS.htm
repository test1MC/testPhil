Economic Development Specialist Responsibilities

  • Contact the district examiner within 30 days of enrollment, and then 10 business days prior to actually starting work to establish communication and begin dialogue of issues involved in the request
  • Discuss relevant DOR items, Examiner's Findings, etc. with the examiner
  • Provide the examiner with a draft of the NWRP (if applicable) and request feedback prior to completing the contact
  • Complete a written Contact Report and upload it in AIRES
