Credit Unions Eligible for the OSCUI Consulting Program

OSCUI’s consulting program provides individualized training and assistance to credit unions on a variety of operational and strategic management issues. NCUA Instruction 6054 (Rev. 3), OSCUI Consulting Program, establishes guidelines for the OSCUI consulting program. Consulting services are available to the following federal and federally insured state-chartered credit unions:

  • Small credit unions, as defined by Interpretive Ruling and Policy Statement 13‑1, Minority Depository Institution Preservation Program
  • Low income designated credit unions
  • Minority Depository Institutions
  • Newly chartered credit unions in operation less than ten years
  • Credit unions with less than $10 million in assets requesting assistance with drafting a net worth restoration plan (NWRP)

OSCUI also provides consulting services to credit union organizing groups with an approved field of membership from the NCUA. EDSs will guide the organizing group through developing their business plans, and completing the chartering application.
