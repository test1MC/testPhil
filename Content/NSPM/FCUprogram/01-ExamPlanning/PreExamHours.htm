Pre-Exam Planning Hours

Field staff will use WCC 94, Pre-Exam Planning FCU, on weekly time reports to capture time spent pre-planning a federal credit union examination. These hours are separate from, and in addition to, the budgeted hours for examining a credit union. Field staff will document their pre-planning activities with the Pre-Exam Planning Questionnaire and upload it with the exam.

Pre-exam hours must be recorded for each completed WCC 10 exam. Exceptions to the budgeted time must be approved in writing by a supervisor, in advance.

The Pre-Exam Planning Questionnaire is available in the AIRES Scope module.
