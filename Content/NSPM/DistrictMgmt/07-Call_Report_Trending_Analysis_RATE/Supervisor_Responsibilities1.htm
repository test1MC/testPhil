Supervisor Responsibilities

  • Review RATE reports submitted by examiners as follows:
    • 100% of assigned credit unions for June and December cycles
    • Random sample of assigned credit unions for March and September cycles
      • Regional Directors will ensure the random sample size is meaningful, and will determine specific sampling criteria.
  • Notify the ARDP of any specific credit unions where supervision plans materially changed as a result of the RATE reviews, necessitating material budget increases for supervision
