Minimal AIRES Upload

AIRES allows for a “Minimal Contact,” for uploading contact reports with limited information. This feature is available for off-site supervision. The minimum information required for a minimal contact is:

  • CAMEL ratings
  • Financial data
  • Time
  • Completed information tab

If an examiner chooses to download and import historical data, the EMC will display the imported information and the examiner will need to enter the CAMEL ratings in the Exam.xls. Additional information regarding minimal contacts can be found in the .
